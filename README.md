# Open Garden

A digital garden where tech knowledge grows, evolves, and stays open for everyone to explore.

## The Vision

Open Garden isn't just another tech blog—it's a living space where ideas take root, grow, and evolve over time. Unlike traditional blogs that freeze content in time, a digital garden embraces the natural process of learning: notes get refined, projects mature, and connections form between seemingly unrelated topics.

## Why a Garden?

- **Content grows** — Ideas start as small notes and develop into full-fledged posts
- **Evolution over perfection** — Posts are updated as understanding deepens
- **Open by design** — Knowledge should be shared freely, not locked behind paywalls
- **Connection matters** — Topics link together, creating a web of understanding
- **Timeless yet current** — Evergreen content that stays relevant through updates

## What You'll Find Here

### 🌱 Seeds (Notes)
Quick thoughts, learning notes, and half-baked ideas. These are the seeds that might grow into something bigger—or stay as useful reference points.

### 🌿 Growing (Writing)
Deeper explorations of topics I'm actively learning about. These posts evolve as my understanding changes.

### 🌳 Mature (Projects)
Completed work and polished tutorials. These represent topics I've explored thoroughly and want to share with others.

### 🔗 Connections (Bookmarks)
Interesting finds from around the web that connect to what I'm learning. The garden extends beyond my own writing.

## The Philosophy

Traditional tech blogs often present a finished product—polished, perfect, and static. But real learning is messy, iterative, and ongoing. Open Garden embraces this reality:

- **Learning in public** — Showing the process, not just the result
- **Iterative improvement** — Posts get better over time
- **Cross-pollination** — Ideas from different fields inform each other
- **Community benefit** — What I learn might help someone else on their journey

## About the Name

"Open Garden" reflects two core values:
- **Open** — All content is freely accessible. No paywalls, no email walls, no friction.
- **Garden** — Content is alive, growing, and interconnected. Not a static archive but a living ecosystem.

## Growing Together

This garden thrives on connection. Whether you're:
- A developer learning a new technology
- Someone exploring career transitions in tech
- Just curious about how things work

There's something here for you. And if you find something useful, share it. Gardens are meant to be enjoyed by everyone.
